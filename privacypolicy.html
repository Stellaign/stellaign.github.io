Privacy Policy for Stellaign

Effective Date: October 2, 2025

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Stellaign (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, www.stellaign.com (the “Site”).

This Policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable laws.

By using our Site, you agree to the practices outlined in this Policy. For questions, contact us at myprivacy@stellaign.com.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data (GDPR): Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Personal Information (CCPA): Information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with a consumer or household.

3. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Subject and Message

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, device details
  • Pages visited, referring URLs, and access times
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics
  • Hosting logs via GitHub Pages and GoDaddy

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent: When you provide details in our contact form.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve, secure, and maintain our Site.
  • Legal Obligation: When processing is necessary to comply with laws.

5. Cookies

We use cookies solely for analytics purposes (via Google Analytics) to monitor site traffic and performance. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

6. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries (consent).
  • To analyze and improve our Site (legitimate interest).
  • To comply with legal obligations (legal requirement).

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may only be shared with:

  • Google Analytics (analytics insights, subject to Google’s Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages and GoDaddy (for hosting and domain services)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU/EEA residents):

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (“Right to be Forgotten”)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request portability of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with a Data Protection Authority

Under CCPA (California residents):

  • Know what categories of personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (see Section 9)
  • Exercise rights without discrimination

9. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information (CCPA)

We want to make it clear:

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your personal information for targeted advertising or cross-context behavioral advertising.

California residents may still opt out of any future sale or sharing of their personal data.

To exercise your rights, contact us at myprivacy@stellaign.com with the subject line: “Privacy Request”.

10. Data Security and Retention

  • Security: We use SSL/TLS encryption and industry-standard safeguards. However, no system is completely secure.
  • Retention: Contact form data is stored only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry, after which it is deleted.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in other countries, including the United States, where our providers (Google, GitHub) operate. We rely on their safeguards and standard contractual clauses where applicable.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. Significant changes will be highlighted on our Site.
